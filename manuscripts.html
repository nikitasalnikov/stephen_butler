Manuscripts

If early text and illuminated manuscripts are your thing, then you have come to the right place. They are our passion as well. We have handled Coptic texts from as early as C5th through to the ‘end days’ of medieval manuscripts in the C15th, and much in between. We buy for all pockets and tastes so hopefully you will find something that stirs your cocoa. And, if not, then remember to sign up to our emails in order to be among the first to see new material.

Please check back soon, our inventory is growing all the time.
