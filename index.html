Welcome to Stephen Butler Rare Books & Manuscripts

Our collections include Medieval and Renaissance manuscripts and early printed Continental and English books. You will also find later manuscript texts and other books of related interest, such as bibliography.

VIEW BOOKS VIEW MANUSCRIPTS VIEW ILLUMINATIONS

Books

On our shelves you will discover early printed English (STC) books together with Continental pre- and post ‘incunabula’. Many of these are in their original or early bindings. Some are illuminated or hand coloured.

VIEW BOOKS

Manuscripts

In our site you will find medieval manuscripts, both text as well as illuminated from a wide date range. We have handled early Coptic texts from as early as C5th through to the ‘end days’ of manuscripts in the C15th.
