Stephen Butler Rare Books & Manuscripts

Stephen Butler has been dealing with books and manuscripts for nearly three decades and has a wide range of interests and expertise. These range from early medieval manuscripts to illuminated manuscripts, cuttings and miniatures through to early printed books, both Continental and English.

We count amongst our clients individual collectors and connoisseurs as well as international Institutional Libraries and galleries.
